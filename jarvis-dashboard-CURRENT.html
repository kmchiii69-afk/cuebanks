Context: the $197/mo low-ticket recurring was deliberately sunset for high-ticket one-time. Read the data through that lens.
Recurring was sunset on purpose
The 4,760 ended memberships are the legacy $197/mo base winding down by design, not churn-as-failure. Crypto audiences quit hard in bear markets — recurring was the wrong model for them.
The pivot is working
One-time revenue ramped ~4.5× Jan→Apr ($9k → $41k) as high-ticket replaced recurring. April's $51k was your best month ever — the new model can out-earn the old one.
But high-ticket is lumpy by nature
